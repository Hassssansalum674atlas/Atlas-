## Long Form
OSHA = Occupational Safety and Health Administration

## Technology
- Mobile-first computer vision (YOLOv8) for real-time hazard detection
- Edge AI capable on smartphones or existing smart glasses
- Dataset covering 28 safety and PPE categories
- Offline-first operation (no internet required for core functionality)
- SDK integration for continuous background operation

## Vision
To make construction sites and industrial workplaces safer by providing real-time AI hazard detection, guidance, and alerts.

## Functionality
- Detect personal protective equipment (PPE)
- Identify scaffolding hazards, unstable surfaces, and falling actions
- Provide real-time visual/audio alerts
- Record and log incidents for review and training
- Trainer Mode: guides users through proper safety procedures
- Working Mode: real-time task support with hazard alerts

## Unique Value
- First portable, offline-capable AI safety assistant
- Combines proactive guidance with real-world execution
- Low-cost, modular, and easily expandable to new industries
- Can operate on existing devices (phones or smart glasses)

## Technology
- Edge AI node with embedded sensors for voltage, load, and anomaly detection
- Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) for secure, tamper-proof data logging
- Predictive algorithms for energy balancing, theft detection, and maintenance alerts
- Compatible with solar, wind, and hybrid microgrid systems
- Offline-first operation with optional cloud synchronization for analytics

## Vision
To provide reliable, intelligent microgrid management that reduces energy loss, prevents theft, and ensures stable electricity delivery in off-grid and rural environments.

## Functionality
- Detect and alert for energy theft
- Predict renewable energy output for load balancing
- Monitor transformers and wiring for predictive maintenance
- Provide real-time dashboards for operators (via phone or tablet)
- Trainer Mode: guides field technicians on maintenance and troubleshooting
- Working Mode: supports real-time grid operation decisions

## Unique Value
- Combines security, reliability, and efficiency in a single compact device
- Edge-first AI reduces dependency on cloud connectivity
- Modular design allows integration with existing microgrid infrastructure
- Reduces energy losses and operational costs for small and medium utilities

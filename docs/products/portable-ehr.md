## Technology
- Embedded edge AI for offline diagnostics and patient record management
- Local PostgreSQL database with AES-256 encryption for privacy
- Non-invasive diagnostic modules (e.g., anemia detection via camera images)
- Compatible with battery-powered devices and low-resource environments
- Optional cloud integration for backup and analytics

## Vision
To empower community health workers and clinics in areas with unreliable power or internet, providing secure, portable, and AI-assisted health record and diagnostic solutions.

## Functionality
- Store and manage patient records offline
- Perform AI-driven diagnostics on-site
- Record images, measurements, and observations securely
- Trainer Mode: guides health workers through procedures
- Working Mode: real-time diagnostic support
- Generate local reports and alerts for urgent conditions

## Unique Value
- Offline-first, privacy-compliant AI health solution
- Reduces dependency on centralized servers
- Portable and easy to use in low-resource environments
- Extensible for future diagnostic modules and telemedicine features

## Status
- Concept documented
- Ready for prototype design and pilot testing
